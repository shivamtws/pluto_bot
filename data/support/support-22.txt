What Goliath Server version am I running?
This article includes the steps to follow in order to determine what version of the Goliath Performance Monitor server is running in your environment.

Connect to the Goliath console
In the top right hand corner, click the Settings link
Once on the Settings page, you will see a section labeled Licensing & Support. In that section, click the link for Version & License Count
A pop-up box will appear listing the version of Goliath that is running
